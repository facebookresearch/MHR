Momentum Body

The Momentum Team

Abstract

We present Momentum Body, a parametric human body model that combines the decoupled skeleton/shape paradigm of ATLAS with a flexible, modern rig and pose corrective system inspired by the Momentum library. Our model enables expressive, anatomically plausible human animation, supporting both linear and non-linear pose correctives, and is designed for robust integration in AR/VR and graphics pipelines.
